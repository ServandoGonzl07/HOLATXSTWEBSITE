FAQs

When will I get my membership? ▶

You will receive your membership details within 3 to 5 business days after registration.

Can i bring other people to the events? ▶

Yes!, except for parties where they'd have to buy a ticket to get in.

How long does the membership last? ▶

The membership lasts one semester only. You have to renew the membership at the start of every semester.
